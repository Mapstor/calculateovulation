Quick Fertility Facts

  • The average menstrual cycle is 28 days, but cycles from 21-35 days are considered normal.
  • Ovulation typically occurs about 14 days before your next period starts.
  • The fertile window spans approximately 6 days: the 5 days before ovulation and ovulation day itself.
  • Sperm can survive in the reproductive tract for up to 5 days, while the egg lives for 12-24 hours after release.
